Why PortaShop?

When building a website you need to make many modifications to the site. It's almost a headache if you have slower or unstable internet connection. Also the server might crash or some errors would cause the entire website crash.
This is why you should learn website development in your local server.

After completing all works, you can upload the entire site to your hosting.
